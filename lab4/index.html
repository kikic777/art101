Lab 4 - Psuedocoding

Making a Reduction Wood Cut:

1a. Sketch out your drawing
1b. Decided where your layers will be within your piece
2. Use tracing paper to transfer your drawing to the wood panel
3. Carve out your first layer (what you carve will be white)
4. Choose and prep your paper
5a. Decide on your first color
5b. Find the relief ink
5c. Mix to your liking
6a. Roll up the wood panel
6b. Spread a band of ink on the table
6c. Roll it out with the brayer
7. Set the pressure of the printing press
8a. Print your first layer
8b. Put newsprint and blotter paper to protect the press from getting dirty
9. Do some ghost prints to remove excess ink
10. Carve your next layer
11. Wait until the ink is dry on your prints before repeating the printing process with a new ink color
12. Repeat until satisfied
13. Clean your tools and station when finished

Flappy Bird

1. Print start screen
2. Allow user input
3. The user clicks a button to start the game
4a. Mouse click makes the bird fly through obstacles
4b. The bird falls by itself when nothing happen
4c. Clicking makes the bird fly upward
5. Randomize the pathway in each game
6. Count the number of sucessful passes
7a. If the bird crashes you lose the game!
7b. Print game over screen
8. Record data of high score for the user
