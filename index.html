About Me

Hi! I'm a third year Art major and Theatre minor at UC Santa Cruz. I'm focusing on illustration, animation, and costuming. Although, I try to work in as many mediums as I can learn, in both traditional and digital formats. As an artist, I am fascinated by the ways different art mediums can come together and work in harmony to create a bigger piece. I'm also a big fan of cartoons and comic books!

You can follow my art on Instagram at @kikic777, where I do a combination of original works and fan art!
