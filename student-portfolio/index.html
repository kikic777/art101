Cynthia Ramirez - Senior Exit Portfolio

Artist Statement

This portfolio is first and foremost an appreciation for being a beginner. It is a celebration of exploration and trying something new. It is a reminder to keep pursuing your dreams and continuing to learn for the love of it. After all, the word amateur is derived from the French word, meaning “the one who loves.”

In trying something new, we not only develop skills, but we learn something about ourselves. While I had always enjoyed animated films and media, I had not realized how much I wanted to pursue it as a career until I tried my first animation course. I still have a long way to go, though. While the transition between graduation and entering the workforce can feel daunting, I want to take the time to appreciate the stage I am at right now. There is something about that hunger to develop a new skill or to pursue an overly ambitious goal. I am at the beginning of my development, still looking to grow, evolve and learn. I know that this stage will bring a lot of failure and frustration, but those are the most underrated moments for growth.

This is a collection of work from the animation courses I took during my Junior and Senior years at UC Santa Cruz. They reflect my journey as a beginner in animation exploring different types of mediums, developing beyond basic skills to create my own stories. Over these two years, I have explored 2D animation, rotoscope, stop motion animation, and have just started exploring 3D modeling and animation.

As I expanded my knowledge over these two years and began to create my stories, I have also reflected on what I wanted them to be about. Many of my works drew from my personal experiences. They mostly revolve around themes of identity, self exploration, and self expression. While they tended to be whimsical and lighthearted, some contained the bittersweetness of uncertainty. My identity as a queer Latina with a chronic condition has influenced some of these themes, but I feel they are universally relatable and can reach anyone.
